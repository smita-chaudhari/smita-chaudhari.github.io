About Me

Hello, I'm Smita

I believe that the most powerful business decisions are hidden inside messy data. As a Data Engineer and Consultant, my passion is simply finding those insights.

My Expertise & Mission

With over 6 years of experience spanning AWS, Azure, and GCP, my mission is to build intelligent, cloud-native platforms that make data work harder for the business. I don't just build pipelines; I bridge the gap between engineering and analytics, designing everything from real-time Spark and Snowflake ETL to custom automation solutions using Python, Airflow, and NLP.

I've tackled high-stakes projects at organizations like IBM, Cognizant, and Arizona State University, always focused on enabling teams to save costs, scale faster, and make smarter, data-backed decisions.

Beyond the Code & What's Next

When I'm not engineering solutions, I'm an explorer at heart finding new trails to hike, listening to classical music, or simply documenting the small stories I find while traveling.

I'm looking for a full-time opportunity where I can continue to learn, solve meaningful problems, and help an organization write its next great data story.
